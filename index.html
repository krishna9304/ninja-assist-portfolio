Ninja Assist

Safety never goes out of hand

So, our initial plan was to create a software based solution to help the covid patients who lose their lives because of negligence of their caretakers. But then we thought, why don't we create a hardware based solution(IoT) while integrating with a software that will make our product more accessible and handy for the people who really need it. So, we created our "wearable" Ninja Assist.

Our Theme(Help and Support)

We developed an IoT based solution, that helps patients to connect with their caretakers without really doing anything. Our product is an arduino based assistant that calls the patient's caretakers in case of any emergency like low blood pressure, fast pulse rate etc.

How it works

Our idea is IoT based device, which works on HTTP trigger.

Emergency Trigger

Ninja Assist creates a trigger when pulse rate of the patient crosses the normal range and sends an HTTP request to the server.

Backend Server

Our backend server then identifies the HTTP request as an emergency and phone calls the patient's caretakers through a voice API and creates an automated message.

Direct Phone Call

Then the caretakers can respond accordingly and call the ambulance or look for hospital beds, donors etc.
